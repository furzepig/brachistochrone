A four-part campaign by Dennis Detwiller. An interconnected series of investigations that can also be run as standalones. Part 1 is set in Hellbend, California, where decades ago an explosion levelled the Hunt Electrodynamics Plant and the town is nearly dead. The campaign deals with Permian-era unnatural forces returning to threaten the present.
