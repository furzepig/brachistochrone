A Delta Green campaign set in Mosul, Iraq in 2016 during brutal occupation by ISIL. From a small base at the Kirkuk airfield, Agents must research the horrors to come and prepare for a harrowing infiltration into territory held by one of history's most evil organisations — and something far worse behind it.
