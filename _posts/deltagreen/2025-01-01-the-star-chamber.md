Delta Green's Task Force T.I. were in Myanmar, deep in the undeveloped hinterlands. The operation went south, people died, and something unnatural and monstrous escaped. A new team must hear testimony from the survivors, decide what happened and who is responsible, and recommend a course of action — playing out the horrific events in flashback.
