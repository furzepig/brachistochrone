---
layout: poem
title: "On The Hana Highway"
poet: "John Hulme"
composed: 2009
category: poems
---

basalt  
asphalt  
looped on the shore  
knife edge switch backs  
nose to tailbacks  
waterfalls  
weed choked  
dribble  
into foam slicked pools  
crushed styrene  
dust and ash  
sunlight  
taillight
