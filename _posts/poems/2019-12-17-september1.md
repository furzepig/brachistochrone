---
layout: poem
category: poems
composed: 1939
title: "September 1, 1939"
poet: W H Auden
---

<div class="ll">I sit in one of the dives</div>    
<div class="ll">On Fifty-second Street</div>    
<div class="ll">Uncertain and afraid</div>    
<div class="ll">As the clever hopes expire</div>    
<div class="ll">Of a low dishonest decade:</div>    
<div class="ll">Waves of anger and fear</div>    
<div class="ll">Circulate over the bright</div>    
<div class="ll">And darkened lands of the earth,</div>    
<div class="ll">Obsessing our private lives;</div>    
<div class="ll">The unmentionable odour of death</div>    
<div class="ll">Offends the September night.</div>    
<div class="ll">&nbsp;</div>  
<div class="ll">Accurate scholarship can</div>    
<div class="ll">Unearth the whole offence</div>    
<div class="ll">From Luther until now</div>    
<div class="ll">That has driven a culture mad,</div>    
<div class="ll">Find what occurred at Linz,</div>    
<div class="ll">What huge imago made</div>    
<div class="ll">A psychopathic god:</div>    
<div class="ll">I and the public know</div>    
<div class="ll">What all schoolchildren learn,</div>    
<div class="ll">Those to whom evil is done</div>    
<div class="ll">Do evil in return.</div>    
<div class="ll">&nbsp;</div>  
<div class="ll">Exiled Thucydides knew</div>    
<div class="ll">All that a speech can say</div>    
<div class="ll">About Democracy,</div>    
<div class="ll">And what dictators do,</div>    
<div class="ll">The elderly rubbish they talk</div>    
<div class="ll">To an apathetic grave;</div>    
<div class="ll">Analysed all in his book,</div>    
<div class="ll">The enlightenment driven away,</div>    
<div class="ll">The habit-forming pain,</div>    
<div class="ll">Mismanagement and grief:</div>    
<div class="ll">We must suffer them all again.</div>    
<div class="ll">&nbsp;</div>   
<div class="ll">Into this neutral air</div>    
<div class="ll">Where blind skyscrapers use</div>    
<div class="ll">Their full height to proclaim</div>    
<div class="ll">The strength of Collective Man,</div>    
<div class="ll">Each language pours its vain</div>    
<div class="ll">Competitive excuse:</div>    
<div class="ll">But who can live for long</div>    
<div class="ll">In an euphoric dream;</div>    
<div class="ll">Out of the mirror they stare,</div>    
<div class="ll">Imperialism's face</div>    
<div class="ll">And the international wrong.</div>    
<div class="ll">&nbsp;</div>    
<div class="ll">Faces along the bar</div>    
<div class="ll">Cling to their average day:</div>    
<div class="ll">The lights must never go out,</div>    
<div class="ll">The music must always play,</div>    
<div class="ll">All the conventions conspire</div>    
<div class="ll">To make this fort assume</div>    
<div class="ll">The furniture of home;</div>    
<div class="ll">Lest we should see where we are,</div>    
<div class="ll">Lost in a haunted wood,</div>    
<div class="ll">Children afraid of the night</div>    
<div class="ll">Who have never been happy or good.</div>    
<div class="ll">&nbsp;</div>  
<div class="ll">The windiest militant trash</div>    
<div class="ll">Important Persons shout</div>    
<div class="ll">Is not so crude as our wish:</div>    
<div class="ll">What mad Nijinsky wrote</div>    
<div class="ll">About Diaghilev</div>    
<div class="ll">Is true of the normal heart;</div>    
<div class="ll">For the error bred in the bone</div>    
<div class="ll">Of each woman and each man</div>    
<div class="ll">Craves what it cannot have,</div>    
<div class="ll">Not universal love</div>    
<div class="ll">But to be loved alone.</div>    
<div class="ll">&nbsp;</div>    
<div class="ll">From the conservative dark</div>    
<div class="ll">Into the ethical life</div>    
<div class="ll">The dense commuters come,</div>    
<div class="ll">Repeating their morning vow;</div>    
<div class="ll">'I will be true to the wife,</div>    
<div class="ll">I'll concentrate more on my work,'</div>    
<div class="ll">And helpless governors wake</div>    
<div class="ll">To resume their compulsory game:</div>    
<div class="ll">Who can release them now,</div>    
<div class="ll">Who can reach the deaf,</div>    
<div class="ll">Who can speak for the dumb?</div>    
<div class="ll">&nbsp;</div>     
<div class="ll">All I have is a voice</div>    
<div class="ll">To undo the folded lie,</div>    
<div class="ll">The romantic lie in the brain</div>    
<div class="ll">Of the sensual man-in-the-street</div>    
<div class="ll">And the lie of Authority</div>    
<div class="ll">Whose buildings grope the sky:</div>    
<div class="ll">There is no such thing as the State</div>    
<div class="ll">And no one exists alone;</div>    
<div class="ll">Hunger allows no choice</div>    
<div class="ll">To the citizen or the police;</div>    
<div class="ll">We must love one another or die.</div>    
<div class="ll">&nbsp;</div>    
<div class="ll">Defenceless under the night</div>    
<div class="ll">Our world in stupor lies;</div>    
<div class="ll">Yet, dotted everywhere,</div>    
<div class="ll">Ironic points of light</div>    
<div class="ll">Flash out wherever the Just</div>    
<div class="ll">Exchange their messages:</div>    
<div class="ll">May I, composed like them</div>    
<div class="ll">Of Eros and of dust,</div>    
<div class="ll">Beleaguered by the same</div>    
<div class="ll">Negation and despair,</div>    
<div class="ll">Show an affirming flame.</div>
