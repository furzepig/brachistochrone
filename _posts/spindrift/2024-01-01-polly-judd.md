title: "Polly Judd"
---
Polly Judd is the protagonist of *Spindrift*. She is a student aboard the generation ship, the daughter of Angharad Judd and granddaughter of Rhys and Varvara. Her father and older brother Alun were killed in a hub motor accident four years before the story begins.

Polly has dark curly hair which she wears in a big ponytail tied with luminous green elastic. She carries a silver locket that tells the time in a small voice. Her ambition is to become a Colony Pathfinder when Spindrift reaches Gloucester.
